What it is

A journal built for letting go

Most journaling apps are built to help you remember. Ember is built to help you release. Write what's weighing on you — then burn it. The act of intentional deletion is surprisingly powerful.

The burn ritual

A breath before you release

Before an entry burns, a short breathing exercise gives you a moment to be present. It's the difference between deleting a file and actually letting something go. The ritual is the point.

Privacy by design

Everything stays on your device

No account. No cloud sync. No data collection. Your entries are encrypted on your device and never leave it. Not even we can read what you write — and that's intentional.
